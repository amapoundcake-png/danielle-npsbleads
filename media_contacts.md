## HOW TO USE THIS LIST

- **For a press pitch:** Target Essence, Refinery29, or Cosmo for body image + representation angles. Lead with the Institute for Body Image and the Vogue + Harvard + women's shelters combination — that is the rare story.
- **For creator economy coverage:** Taylor Lorenz, Sydney Bradley, Amanda Mull, and Kaitlyn Tiffany are the names. A story about building a creator brand without a cosign, or the economics of being a mid-size creator, fits their beats perfectly.
- **For speaking/thought leadership:** Bloomberg Businessweek (Amanda Mull) and The Atlantic (Kaitlyn Tiffany) are the right home for longer-form essays or expert quotes.
- **For brand partnership angles:** Business Insider's creator economy reporters (Sydney Bradley, Dan Whateley) cover brand deals, platform strategy, and creator business models.
