About

Danni Adams is a media personality, cultural storyteller, and body image advocate who has built what most brands spend millions trying to manufacture: a community that actually trusts her. Through @amapoundcake, she has created a space where women see themselves honestly represented, and they show up for it every single day.

Her credibility is not limited to a follower count. She is the Co-Creator of the Institute for Body Image, a professional development program that trains medical providers in inclusive, body-positive care. She has been featured in Vogue, appeared on The Jennifer Hudson Show, Tamron Hall, Fox News, Dr. Phil, and TLC, and has partnered with brands from T-Mobile and Hilton Hotels to YITTY by Lizzo and Morphe Beauty.

When a brand works with Danni, they are not buying a post. They are accessing a community built on honesty, on showing up as a real person in a world of highlight reels, and on the belief that representation is not a trend. It is a responsibility. Beyond her media work, she regularly speaks at women's shelters and girls' mentoring programs, the same conversations about self-confidence, resilience, and dreaming big that she brings to university stages, just in different rooms.

Her audience does not just follow her. They believe her. That is the difference.
